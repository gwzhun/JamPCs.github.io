Legion Slim 5

The Legion Slim 5, enabling you to power up your gaming & creative passions with this AMD Ryzen™ 7000 series-powered gaming laptop featuring NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 40 Series graphics.


Fierce flair with fine-tuned function

Lean and mean measuring as thin as 0.78″ and under 5.07lbs, the Lenovo Legion Slim 5 features a fierce chassis that doesn't skimp on style or strength. The design of this laptop beautifully balances form and function with a durable exterior for enhanced durability and mechanical resistance, while housing an ultra-powerful engine. Available in Storm Grey or Misty Grey*, and loaded with features like an up-to 1080p webcam, rear I/O dashboard, and SD card reader, the Lenovo Legion Slim 5 is designed for the bold and fearless.


Gear up for gameplay & content creation

Get ready for pro-level gameplay and content creation with the Lenovo Legion Slim 5 laptop's 16″ WQXGA Lenovo PureSight Gaming display with a 16:10 aspect ratio. This large screen boasts up to a lightning-fast 240Hz refresh rate, NVIDIA® G-SYNC®, and AMD FreeSync™ Premium support for ultra-smooth gameplay. Plus, its 100% sRGB color accuracy, 500 nits of brightness, and HDR 400 support deliver crystal-clear visuals. And with X-rite technology, you'll get a remarkable wide and accurate color gamut and massive resolution to fully immerse yourself in your gameplay or content canvas.
