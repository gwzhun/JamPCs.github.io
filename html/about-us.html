About Us

JamPCs is a company dedicated to fulfilling our customer's desire and demand for personalized and customized PCs. Founded in 2022 by a group of fresh-graduates, JamPCs seeks to provide the services that they themselves hoped to have when they were, catering to an ever-growing audience of users and enthusiasts today.

Experience the JamPCs Difference

With a team of passionate enthusiasts and a devotion to providing the best possible experience with PCs, this company stands as a reliable and assured choice for anybody seeking personalized and high-performance PCs and laptops. From personalizing almost every aspect, to nationwide delivery and reliable maintenance services, JamPCs aims to redefine customer interaction with technology and elevate their computing experience to new heights.

Personalization at the Zenith

JamPCs prides itself on providing a platform which allows customers to fully customize their desired specifications in their PCs or laptops. From high-end components for more demanding and power-intensive tasks, to more affordable options for everyday use, JamPCs offers a wide range of products to be able to serve customers of any and all backgrounds. With such a wide array of selections available, each PC or laptop becomes an extension of the user themselves, reflecting their preferences and requirements, ensuring a truly personalized experience.

Customer-centric Maintenance Services

JamPCs not only delivers top-notch PCs and laptops but also extends its dedication to customer satisfaction through attentive maintenance services. Customers are able to request for maintenance services by meeting us in stores or by emailing us and shipping their device back to the company. During the valid warranty period, customers incur no costs or additional charges* for maintenance services provided. Even after the warranty period has expired, JamPCs continues to support its customers with maintenance services, ensuring their devices remain in peak condition while incurring nominal costs.

Nationwide Coverage and Hassle-Free Shipping

Based in Pulau Pinang, Malaysia, JamPCs offers country-wide coverage, delivering purchased products to all parts of the country right to your doorstep. The company takes pride in its transparent approach to pricing, as shipping costs as well as service taxes are included in the billing issued to customers, ensuring no hidden costs are issued. Shipping is carried out by trusted local courier service providers, guaranteeing a hassle-free delivery for all customers.
