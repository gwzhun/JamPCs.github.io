Frequently asked questions

Have questions? We’re here to help

When will JamPCs start to process my order?

JamPCs will process all orders immediately once we have received the payment of the total cost of your order.

Are the batteries removable?

Depending on the model as most of our batteries are built into the machine. Therefore, the only way to remove them is to unscrew the back panel.

Can I cancel the order for a refund after paying the full payment?

Because all laptops and PCs are made to order, once the payment has been processed, there will be no refunds

Do I have to buy Windows for my Gaming Computer?

You do not have to buy Windows operating system, but it is definitely the best one for gaming.
Almost all games made are compatible with windows – the same is not true for MacOS or Linux. Linux computers can work fine, but you won’t have as many options as on Windows. A vast majority of gamers use Windows and you’ll get the most support if you go with Windows. I personally use Windows for all PC gaming activities.

Is there a fee for repair/maintence?

For issues covered under the Warranty and within the warranty period, there will be no additional fees.
For out-of-warranty services, the repair cost is subject to the problem diagnosed. A minimum of RM 100 will be charged as diagnosis fee. This diagnosis fee can be waived if repair is done.

Do I need more than 8GB of RAM?

If it’s in your budget, it would be highly recommended to run more than 8GB of RAM. 12-16GB is ideal for most gaming desktops, but 8 GB will still run most games well.

What is the benefit of custom PC?

This means faster loading times when gaming or running memory intensive programs such as video editing software.
Custom PCs also tend to be more reliable than pre-built models since they're designed with specific tasks in mind, meaning fewer hardware issues down the line.
